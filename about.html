About Me:

Hi! My name is Laura Lennon and im from Plymouth Meeting, Penslyvania. I went to high school at Germantown Academy and I am now a Sophomore at George Washington University apart of the Milken School of Public Health majoring in Public Health and minoring in Business and Criminal Justice. My expected graduation date in June 2024. I hope to one day get a job in Healthcare Administration.

High School Activities:

In high school my main activities included being a doubles player on the Varsity Girls Tennis Team and being an Editor and Photographer for the School Yearbook. I also participated in Patriot Ambassadors where I gave tours of the school to perspective students and their families. One after school program participated in frequently was an after school tutoring program where I would travel to Philadelphia to tutor children from our schools Community Partnership School. I was also lucky enough to get to travel to Poland and across Europe with my schools Choir.

College Activities:

In college I currently am apart of the sorority Sigma Delta Tau and a club called GlobeMed. Within my sorority a held a previous position on Co-Chair of Diversity, Equity, and Enclusion and this year I just became the new, and first, Vice President of Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion. In Globemed I am apart of the Outreach Committee where I help reach out to other clubs to spread the word about GlobeMed's philanthropy and possibly partner in hosting an event.
